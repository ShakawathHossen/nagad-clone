Nagad ( ডাক বিভাগের ডিজিটাল লেনদেন )

Nagad (নগদ) is a Bangladeshi Digital Financial Service (DFS), operating under the authority of Bangladesh Post Office,an attached department of the Ministry of Post and Telecommunication (Bangladesh). It is the new version of the previously introduced Postal Cash Card and Electronic Money Transfer System (EMTS) of the Bangladesh Post Office.Nagad headquarters located at Kemal Atatürk Avenue in Dhaka, Bangladesh.
